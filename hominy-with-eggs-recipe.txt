Ingredients:
 - 1 lb cooked hominy, can also use canned hominy
 - 2 tbs butter
 - 4 eggs
 - 1 cup of chopped leeks (white part only) or white onion
 - 2 garlic cloves, crushed
 - 1/4 tsp ground achiote
 - 1/4 cup milk
 - 2 tbs chopped chives
 - 1 tbs finely chopped cilantro or parsley
 - Salt to taste

Directions:
 1. Heat the butter over medium heat in a large saut√© pan, add the chopped 
    leeks or onions, crushed garlic, achiote and salt to make a refrito, 
    cook until the leeks are soft, about 5 minutes.
 2. Add the hominy, stir in well and cook for another 2 minutes.
 3. Add the milk and cook until the milk is almost all absorbed by the 
    hominy.
 4. Whisk the eggs and add them to the hominy, stir well and cook for about
    3-5 minutes.
 5. Stir in the chives and cilantro and add additional salt if needed.
 6. Serve accompanied by fresh cheese slices and hot black coffee.
